Hobbies

Language Learner

I am very scholarly when it comes to learning foreign languages and I love learning about the culture and customs that resulted in the language's contemporary form. Getting to know people from a different country and being able to communicate with them hase opened my eyes to different points of view and expanded my horizons.

I especially am fond of languages that use a non-roman alphabet such as Arabic, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Georgian etc. I hope to know at least 10 languages by the time I'm 40 years old.

Electronics Hobbyist

I love computers and everything related to them. I consider them the pinnacle of human creativity and engineering and I'd love to build my very own computer some day with its very own custom operating system and custom made components designed by me.

As a 2000s kid I am fascinated by the rate at which technology is progressing and make it my mission to have the same level of understanding about electronics that old physicists had for electromagnetism. I love the process of creating a circuit, building it and executing it commands on it.

Bookworm

I love reading science books. I most recently have become enamored with the history of contemporary physics and how technology has been affected/benefited so far. I also use book reading as a tool to sharpen my focus and to lengthen my attention span.

Digital Photo and Video Editing

I like to create art and retouch photos of friends and family using GIMP photo editing software, as well as edit videos I make for my YT channel using DaVinci Resolve. I am not an expert on neither yet but I aim to improve my skills and unleash my creativity through them.

Survival Enthusiast

I am a camping enthusiast and therefore I like learning how to survive in the wild. I frequently go for hikes in the nearby mountains and try to spend the night in the wild. I believe such skills are a lost art and should be essential life knowledge for anyone.
