My Favorite Movies

Theodosios's movie recommendations

Spider-Man 2 (2004)

Spider-Man 2 is justifiably considered to be one of the best superhero movies of all time. But not because of the fancy battle scenes or the impressive CGI for its time... because of how it underlines the human struggles we all go through and can relate, accentuated by the responsibility of having to protect people as a superhero.

Toy Story (1995)

Toy Story is an all-time classic by now, which I believe kids are going to be watching again and again for years to come. A buddy-cop movie with elements of true friendship, self-acceptance and teamwork, Toy Story is a serious achievement in storytelling as much as it is a technical marvel in CGI cinema.

Saving Private Ryan

For more mature audiences, I recommend Saving Private Ryan. This movie grounds you to reality like no other. It establishes a world in which real people are pushed to their limit by the reality of war and how they find solace into their comradery and companionship. I personally felt like I was brought along for the ride and I got to explore multi-layered personallities, their struggles and their dreams. Must watch!
